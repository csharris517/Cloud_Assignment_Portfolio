Porter's Five Forces in Practice

This model is widely used in order to evaluate a company's position within its industry and its competitiveness. Although applicable to any industry, it is best used when evaluating factors specific to the company. If the company spans multiple industries, then it should evaluate the Five Forces separately for each industry. There are three basic steps to perform an industry analysis:
