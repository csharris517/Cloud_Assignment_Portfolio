The Industrial Internet of Things


According to Accenture, the Industrial Internet of Things will "transform companies and countries, opening up a new era of economic growth and competitiveness." What they are referring to is the intersection of people, data, and intelligent machines to increase productivity, efficiency, and operations of industries around the world. This industry of creating intelligent systems that work together embodies the Third Wave of Technology in that it is connecting products and people in ways previously unseen.
